Why Secro Exists

The modern internet has a privacy problem. Most platforms treat your data as a product. At Secro, we believe privacy isn't a premium feature—it's a human right.

We started this project to provide a communication tool that requires zero personal data. No phone numbers, no email tracking, and no metadata logging.

Our architecture ensures that you remain anonymous, while your conversations remain entirely yours through end-to-end encryption.
